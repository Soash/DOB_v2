About Us

Pioneering the Future of Computational Biology

Established 2024

Our Journey

Every great revolution begins with a dream. A few years ago, in a small room in Dhaka, we set out to forge the future of computational biology in Bangladesh. Armed with basic laptops and an unshakable belief, Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited was born—proudly standing today as the country’s first bioinformatics limited company and first highly growing startup.
Today, that quiet dream is a dynamic 1,500-square-foot headquarters powered by cutting-edge AI. Operating as a complete scientific ecosystem, we pioneer "Bio-AI" to accelerate workflows in molecular dynamics, NGS, and Metagenomics, bringing landmark projects like the BMPPD to life.
Our true heartbeat is our people: a brilliant core team, active campus coordinators, and a 35,000+ community. Expanding through our new Chittagong branch, our mission is clear: to become South Asia's leading bioinformatics firm within five years. The dawn has truly arrived.

Mission

Our definitive mission is to establish ourselves as the premier bioinformatics authority in South Asia within the next five years. We are committed to fostering a culture of technological rigor and scientific integrity, empowering researchers with the most advanced computational tools available in the global market.

Vision

We envision a future where innovation is universally accessible and biology is entirely data-driven. By democratizing high-throughput analysis and machine learning in life sciences, we aim to accelerate the global transition toward a healthier, more sustainable bio-economy.

The Integrated Ecosystem

At Dawn of Bioinformatics, we have built a seamless integration of services, education, and research. Our ecosystem connects theoretical discovery with industrial application, ensuring that biological data translates directly into real-world progress through our specialized divisions. This interconnected approach allows us to maintain a closed-loop system where academic insights fuel industrial tools, and practical challenges from the field inform our next generation of research.

Our ecosystem is further strengthened by strategic partnerships across the globe, allowing us to leverage international standards while focusing on the unique biodiversity and clinical needs of South Asia. Whether through our high-throughput sequencing services or our intensive professional development modules, Dawn of Bioinformatics serves as the central hub for the regional bio-economy, driving efficiency and excellence in every strand of DNA we analyze.

DBS (Dawn Bioinformatics Services)

Our specialized division providing industry-leading computational analysis and genomic pipelines. We offer end-to-end data processing for research institutions and biotech firms globally, ensuring high-throughput precision in every analysis.

IBAI (Institute of Bioinformatics and Artificial Intelligence)

Empowering the next generation through intensive certifications and practical bioinformatics learning. Our training modules are designed by industry veterans to bridge the skill gap in the South Asian scientific community.

DawniLab R&D

DawniLab is our independent R&D engine dedicated to pushing the boundaries of computational biology. This collaborative hub pioneers new methodologies, publishes high-impact research, and brings landmark projects like the Bangladeshi Medicinal Plant Phytochemicals Database (BMPPD) to life.
